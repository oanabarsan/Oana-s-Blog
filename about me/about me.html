About OANA

My name is Oana-Maria Barsan, I am 26 years old and I live in the heart of Bucovina, in a small town in north-est of Romania called Gura Humorului. I grew up in a small village, near my current town where I can say I had a beautiful childhood.

Personally, I dream about an IT career and I work hard for that. I started by learning alone from online materials and now I am confident about my potential. I believe my perseverance and ambition will help me achieve my goal. I am fast learner, open minded, I can easily go out of the box and very motivated in continuing this journey.

My hobby are not a lot, since my main activity is studying IT, but in my spare time I like going out with my friends, watching Netflix, going to trips and cooking.

Find out more about my IT projects here on my Github profile.

Education

Recently, I graduated a Manual tester course organized my CodersLab in partnership with eJobs. The course lasted almost 4 months and I learnt about Agile methodologies, Software Testing Life cycle, test techniques, test levels, test cases, test plans, defect reports and API testing. It was one of the most interesting course and I really enjoyed taking part to this project.

Before that, back in 2015 I started my bachelor studies at 'Alexandru Ioan Cuza' University, in Iasi, in Human Resources field. During this stage, I studied a lot of subjects, but most of them had nothing to do with human resources.

After graduation, I decided to change the field of study and I chose Regional Development studies, for master degree, at 'European Studies Center' belonging to Faculty of Law, in the same university. Here, I studied a lot of subjects just like in bachelor degree, but the main idea was to learn about european projects.

Professionally

Since august 2020 I have been working as Senior Compliance Associate at Amazon, in a fully remote environment. My main job is to verify compliance documentation for different types of products and decide if a can be still sold on the website or not.Of course, during this period i have been taking part to several projects and accomplished other tasks.

Before Amazon, I worked in different fields, but mostly in my family business and tourism. I started by helping my parents in their business concerning construction materials and later on, I spent 2 summers in Germany to help my father in his business regarding second-hand furniture.

When i had to move in Iasi for Faculty, I wanted to try the Work and Travel experience, so in 2016 I chose to go to SUA, on Mackinac Island, Michigan state. It was one of the hardest experience of my life, but I can say I came stronger and more experienced.There, I worked as landry assistant, dinning room assistant and cash register.

Next year, in 2017 I decided I want to change the country and I chose Greece, Corfu Island. This time, it was the best experience of my life, full of sun and fun. There, I had only one jos which was bar assistant, but beside that I had other tasks as well, in the same hotel.

After deciding that I had enough of Work and Travel experiences, I tried to find a job in Iasi, so I applied to a Manager Assistant position in a Real Estate agency. There, I worked one year and I can say it was one of the easiest job I had.
