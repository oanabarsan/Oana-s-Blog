About OANA's blog

My blog here is to offer tips, ideas, and inspiration for making your "life with dog" easier and more enjoyable. I try to help our readers solve problems and prevent new ones. And of course, we have to weave in a little fun and variety too.

You'll find that I write articles about dog fact and things that dogs are prone to, including life with dogs, fun facts about them and dogs vs cats lives. I try to offer guidance for your doggie health questions and help you sort out your dog's behavior issues with simple, time-proven methods for training your dog. Lots of these articles involve behavior issues that you may find helpful at home.

You can enjoy a free subscription to my blog's posts, allowing you to never miss a single edition, by a method such as email.
